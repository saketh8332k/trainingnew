Understanding the HMPV Virus

Human Metapneumovirus (HMPV) is a respiratory virus that primarily affects young children, the elderly, and immunocompromised individuals. It can cause symptoms ranging from mild cold-like illness to severe respiratory infections.

Symptoms include fever, cough, nasal congestion, and difficulty breathing. In severe cases, it can lead to pneumonia or bronchitis. HMPV has been identified as a major contributor to respiratory illnesses worldwide, with significant impacts on healthcare systems.
